About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm Margaret Khamvongsa, a SaaS developer with over 8 years of experience in the tech industry. My career began with a strong focus on the Microsoft Power Platform, where I honed my skills in building solutions with PowerApps, crafting insightful dashboards with Power BI, and creating efficient backend systems using Dataverse and SQL. I have even dabbled with some custom connectors using c#.

Recently, I expanded my expertise to include modern web development, combining these new skills with my existing experience to create comprehensive and innovative solutions. This website serves as a showcase of my journey, highlighting key projects, accomplishments, and the breadth of my technical abilities.

Whether you're here as a collaborator, a potential employer, or someone exploring the world of software development, you'll find insight into my dedication to solving complex problems, delivering value-driven solutions, and continuously evolving as a developer.
