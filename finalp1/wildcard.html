Exploring the world of competitve dog confirmation and handling

Competitive conformation dog shows are a demanding but rewarding hobby for me. It takes dedication, time, and effort to present Beau, my Papillon, in front of judges and showcase how well he matches the breed standard. From grooming to handling, every detail matters. I love that this hobby lets me support the breed I’m passionate about, even if Beau is currently an "adorable loser" with zero points. Each show is an opportunity for us to improve and grow together. The experience and the sense of community make the effort worthwhile. I’m confident that with persistence and love, Beau’s moment to shine will come. Whether he earns points or not, the journey we’re on together and my dedication to the Papillon breed already make it a win for me.
